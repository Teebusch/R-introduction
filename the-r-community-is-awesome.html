10.2 How to keep learning?

Because R is mainly used by (and taught to) people who are not software engineers or computer scientists, there is a lot of learning material that is made for people without a technical background. Amazingly, much of it is free and really good! Here, I will limit myself to just a few personal recommendations. The (Awesome R Learning Resources List)[https://github.com/iamericfletcher/awesome-r-learning-resources] provides a more comprehensive list of resources for learning R.
