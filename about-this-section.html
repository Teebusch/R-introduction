Ten Reasons to Learn R

About this section

Now that we know what R is, and we have discussed some good alternatives to R, we will have a look at what speaks for learning R. There are many good reasons to learn R! In this section of the book I will present ten of them, and we will also get hands-on with R. This is a very superficial introduction and we will skip over some of the details for the sake of showing you the whole spectrum of what R has to offer and providing you with knowledge that is practically usefuls in as little time as possible.

We will use modern R with RStudio and the Tidyverse (more about that later). This will help us to focus on data analysis quickly, without worrying to much about some of the more technical aspects of programming.
